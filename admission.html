Admissions

Berklee attracts the most creative young musicians in the world who know that no other music college or institution offers such a rich diversity of people, music, and programs. They come here to discover their true music calling; pushing themselves past their own expectations and into the forefront of every aspect of the global music community. We look forward to meeting you and hearing what you will personally bring to our community.

Our educational facilities are designed to meet the needs of around 4,100 students and to maintain an average class size of 11 students. At Berklee, you get the advantages of a small college as well as experience with faculty who are some of the world's most renowned musicians. More students than ever are applying, and enrolled students are continuing on to graduation at record rates. We would like to take this opportunity to invite you to become part of the largest community of musicians.

To get the most accurate and detailed information, please identify what type of prospective student you are by using the side navigation bar.



Audition and Interview

As part of your application to the college, you are required to complete a live audition and interview. All applicants will be automatically considered for scholarships through our live audition and interview process. You may participate in your audition regardless of whether the college has received your supporting materials.



About Scholarships at Berklee

All applicants to the college's full-time degree or diploma programs will be automatically considered for scholarships through our audition and interview process. Continuing students who have completed at least two semesters of full-time study at the college may apply for a scholarship by submitting an achievement portfolio.
